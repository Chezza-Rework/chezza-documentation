<span style={{color: 'red'}}>**Parameters**</span>\
**toggle** - `boolean` - Locks the Phone to prevent opening it and automatically close the Phone.

```lua
exports.phone:lockPhone(toggle)
```

### Example

```lua
exports.phone:lockPhone(true) -- Locks the Phone
exports.phone:lockPhone(false) -- Unlocks the Phone
```
